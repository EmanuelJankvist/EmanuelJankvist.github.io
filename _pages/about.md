Welcome to my academic website, dedicated to accessibility and centralization of contact information. Here, you'll find streamlined access to my contact details, research (when applicable), resources (when applicable), and CV, all in one place. If there is information missing anywhere, feel free to contact me through mail.

A bit about myself.
======
I am currently getting my undergraduate degree in insurance mathematics at Copenhagen University. I plan to take my graduate degree there as well. In my free time I enjoy reading.

Information on the style of website.
======
This website was made by forking the GitHub Pages academicpages template. Nothing major has been changed from the original repository, except for pictures and Markdown documents. This way the website is never overengineered. The hope was for the website to be easily navigable and simplistic.


For more info about the template.
------
More info about academicpages can be found in [the guide](https://academicpages.github.io/markdown/). The [guides for the Minimal Mistakes theme](https://mmistakes.github.io/minimal-mistakes/docs/configuration/) (which this theme was forked from) might also be helpful.
